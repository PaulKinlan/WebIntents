Service

This is a service you use. It has registered its intent to be able to handle the share action for "image/*" and "text/uri-list" datatypes.

Share Intent Registration

<intent 
  action="http://webintents.org/share" 
  type="image/*" 
  href="shareimage.html" 
  title="Kinlan's Image Share" />
<intent 
  action="http://webintents.org/share" 
  type="text/uri-list" 
  href="sharelink.html" 
  title="Kinlan's Link Share" />

Any application that launches a startActivity with an action of "http://webintents.org/share" will see these applications listed.
